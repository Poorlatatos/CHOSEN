# CHOSEN
On an expedition to a long lost kingdom, your journey as the player takes you deep into the depths of a horror that once triumped the lands you walk upon. The Kingdom of Treffenhime was not a prosperous nation but it was caught between many different warring factions and thus suffered the brunt of such horrors of it.  In hopes of regaining their long lost independence they awaken a deity that was forgotten by mankind for their own safety.  Your goal was to exploit the riches from this long lost kingdom but instead you get roped into a long standing feud between several gods. You have been CHOSEN for this, against your will.  You must do whatever you can to either, prevail from the gods, undermine them or work with them to put down a sacred evil that lurks below in the lost kingdom.
